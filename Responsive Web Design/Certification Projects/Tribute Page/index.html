LeBron James

The greatest NBA player of all time.

Team USA's two-time Olympic gold medalist broke the career NBA regular season record held by Kareem Abdul-Jabbar. Basketball hero LeBron James is the NBA's all-time top scorer, but he isn't finished yet.
